The Siege of Leningrad

Animated documentary · After Effects

Winner of the All-Russian animation competition “Animir”
Documentary animation category

This animated documentary tells the story of the Siege of Leningrad through restrained motion, typography and rhythm. The project focuses on emotional accuracy, historical respect and clear visual storytelling.

The animation was fully produced in After Effects — from concept and story structure to timing, compositing and final delivery.
