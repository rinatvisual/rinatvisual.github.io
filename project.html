The Siege of Leningrad

Animated documentary · After Effects

Winner of the All-Russian animation competition “Animir”
Documentary animation category

This animated documentary explores the Siege of Leningrad through restrained motion, typography and rhythm. The focus is on emotional accuracy, historical respect and clear visual storytelling.

The project was fully produced in After Effects — from concept development and narrative structure to animation, compositing and final delivery.
