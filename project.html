The Siege of Leningrad

Animated documentary · Motion design

Winner of the All-Russian animation competition “Animir”
Documentary animation category

This animated documentary explores the Siege of Leningrad through restrained motion, typography and rhythm. The project avoids illustrative excess and focuses on emotional accuracy, historical respect and clear visual storytelling.

The animation was fully produced in After Effects — from concept development and narrative structure to timing, compositing and final delivery.
